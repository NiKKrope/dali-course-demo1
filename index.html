Fun facts about turtles

Some fun facts about turtles

  1. Turtles belong to one of the oldest reptile groups in the world
  2. Just like your bones, a turtle’s shell is actually part of its skeleton. It’s made up of over 50 bones which include the turtle’s rib cage and spine.
  3. Contrary to popular belief, a turtle cannot come out of its shell. The turtle’s shell grows with them, so it’s impossible for them to grow too big for it!
